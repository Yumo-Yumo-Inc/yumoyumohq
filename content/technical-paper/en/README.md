# Yumo Yumo Technical Whitepaper

The Yumo Yumo Technical Paper is the architectural and mechanical specification of the protocol. The Vision Paper carries the market intent and economic narrative; this document explains how receipt processing, the trust layer, reward accounting, data schema, and on-chain settlement work.

The public document contains protocol contracts, data boundaries, schema shapes, and mathematical relationships. Operational thresholds, provider routing policy, defense runbooks, and security parameters are managed in the internal operations layer.

## Scope

- System architecture and data boundaries.
- Stage contracts for the receipt pipeline.
- The public decision surface of the trust and anti-abuse model.
- The working model of tokenomics mechanics and reward accounting.
- Data schema, API surface, and aggregate data layer.
- Risk classes and mitigations explainable at public level.

# Identity and contribution

## Identity and contribution

- **Proof of Expense (PoE)** — The engine that turns a verified receipt into a contribution record. The core protocol primitive of Yumo Yumo. *See: 02 Receipt Pipeline.*
- **Proof of Contribution (PoC)** — The 10% allocation rail that rewards non-receipt contributions: engineering, governance, ecosystem work, and the founding team. The team earns through PoC on the same impact-weighted logic as outside contributors. *See: 04 Tokenomics Mechanics.*
- **Price memory** — The household-level historical price record that Yumo Yumo builds as a user accumulates verified receipts. *See: 02 Receipt Pipeline, 05 Data Schema and API.*
- **Hidden cost** — The decomposed cost components (rent, energy, logistics, tax, margin) of a household's basket, surfaced in the Yumbie app and quantified as ePoints. *See: 05.*
- **Contribution Hub** — The user-facing surface where contributions, level, and rewards are visible. *See: 05.*
- **Yumbie surfaces** — The four product surfaces of the Yumbie app: Home, Payments, Shopping, Goals. *See: 00.*

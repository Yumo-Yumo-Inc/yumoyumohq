# Compliance and legal

## Compliance and legal

- **KVKK** — Türkiye's personal data protection law (Kişisel Verilerin Korunması Kanunu). Equivalent in scope to GDPR. *See: 08.*
- **GDPR** — The EU's General Data Protection Regulation. Applies to EU residents' data regardless of where Yumo Yumo operates. *See: 08.*
- **MASAK** — Türkiye's financial intelligence unit (Mali Suçları Araştırma Kurulu). Relevant for AML and crypto-specific reporting. *See: 08.*
- **MiCA** — Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation, the EU framework for crypto-asset issuance and service provision. Applies from 2024 onwards. *See: 08.*
- **Howey Test** — The US Supreme Court test (SEC v. Howey, 1946) used to determine whether an asset is an "investment contract" and therefore a security. Relevant to INT's US classification. *See: 08.*
- **VAT / KDV** — Value-added tax (Türkiye: Katma Değer Vergisi). Detected on receipts and used as a reconciliation signal. *See: 02.*

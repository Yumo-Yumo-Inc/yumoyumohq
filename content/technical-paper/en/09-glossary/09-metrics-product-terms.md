# Metrics and product terms

## Metrics and product terms

- **MAU** — Monthly Active Users. The denominator for the emission curve scaling band. *See: 04.*
- **Receipt** — In Yumo Yumo, a document representing a household expense. Paper retail receipts, e-invoices, and order confirmations fall into this scope.
- **Verified receipt** — A receipt that falls into the accepted trust-scoring band and earns bINT. *See: 03.*
- **Statistics record** — A document included in household statistics; reward and B2B aggregate scope are determined by the relevant schema rules. *See: 02, 05.*
- **Line item** — A single row on a receipt: raw text + canonical product reference + quantity + price.
- **Review queue** — The queue for borderline receipts routed by the trust layer to manual assessment. *See: 03.*

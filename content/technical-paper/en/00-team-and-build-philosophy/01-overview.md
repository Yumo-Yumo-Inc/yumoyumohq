# 00 — Team and Organization

Yumo Yumo is a protocol developed by Yumo Yumo Inc., a Delaware corporation in the United States. This section of the technical paper describes the responsibility boundary between the company, the core team, and the protocol.

Organization information, custody model, and external support roles are described at public level. Signing procedures, supplier contracts, security runbooks, and internal operational processes are managed in the internal operations layer.

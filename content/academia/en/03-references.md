# References

A consolidated list of the institutional sources and the literature this paper
draws on. Institutional figures are read live in the
[source registry](01-hidden-cost/04-source-registry.md) with their effective dates;
the entries here name the bodies and works.

## 1. Institutional & official sources

| Institution | Used for |
|---|---|
| TÜİK (Turkish Statistical Institute) | Average retail prices; CPI and sub-indices; producer price index; labour-cost indices |
| TCMB (Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye) | Inflation report; index movements for period alignment |
| EPDK (Energy Market Regulatory Authority) | Fuel taxation; distributor margins |
| Ministry of Commerce | Customs-duty decisions on imported goods |
| TZOB (Union of Chambers of Agriculture) | Producer-to-retail produce markups |
| Rekabet Kurumu (Competition Authority) | Grocery gross margins; market concentration |
| SETBİR, SUDER, MEYED, BYSD, UZZK, MOSDER | Dairy, bottled water, juice, vegetable & olive oil, furniture margins |
| KAP (Public Disclosure Platform) | Listed-company gross margins |
| TOBB, İSO, chamber sector reports | Cost-composition weight ranges |
| OECD | Inter-country input-output tables (cross-country cost reference) |
